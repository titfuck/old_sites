Difference between Hubs and Switches

Everyone using ethernet shares a maximum amount of bandwidth that the various ethernet cards multiplex (share). As of this manual's writing, that amount of bandwidth is 10Mbps (about 1.2 MB / sec). With a switch, transfers between two computers that are both in the house receive 10Mbps dedicated to themselves. So if there are lots of large transfers going on in the house (transfering MP3s, video, etc.) then a switch can greatly improve performance. It is a good idea to plug people who have MP3 or video servers into the switch, as well as the webserver, print server, etc.
