Ethernet/Phone Patch Box

Every room has at least one box of ports into with phones or computers can be connected. All these ports lead to the central Patch Box located in the Net Closet on the 2nd floor. This patch box has two sets of ports. On the top half, each port is labeled corresponding to the matching port in a room somewhere in the house. The bottom half is labeled with room telephone extentions (the ones that go 300, 301, etc).

You can plug a number of things into a port in a room (phone, computer, digital phone), so it is your responsibility to appropriately wire the corresponding cables in the closet.

  • Ethernet should be connected from the Patch Box to an ethernet hub.
  • A phone should be connected to the corresponding room extention on the lower half of the Patch Box.
  • Digital Phones should be connected to a room extention designated as Digital.
