Existing mailing lists

The existing mailing lists that you are responsible for are:
  • tdc-webbitch - maintainers of tdc.mit.edu
  • tdc-acl - although you can send mail to this list, it is actually used as a control mechanism. Anyone on this list can edit all the mailing lists, see read all officer lockers, and change permissions on the lockers. No one but the netgeek (and past netgeeks) should be on this list.
  • tdc-YYYY - the brothers in class year YYYY (such as tdc-2002 for the class of 2002)
  • tdc-brothers - the tdc-YYYY lists that correspond to active brothers, plus alumni still interested in the day-to-day TDC activity
  • tdc-pledges - the pledges and the pledge trainer 5
  • tdc - tdc-brothers + tdc-pledges + some alumni that like getting to much email
  • tdc-deadwood - Alumni
  • tdc-local-deadwood - Alumni in the Boston area
  • tdc-seasonal - People in the house during summer or IAP
