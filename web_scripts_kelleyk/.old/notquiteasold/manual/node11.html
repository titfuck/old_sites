Cables

  • Digital Phone and Ethernet cables are both RJ-45. Their ends look like regular phone cords, but are wider.
  • Phone cables are RJ-11, and you have probably seen these before.
  • Cable is coax, and you have probably seen that before as well.

You can buy tips for any of these types of cables at Microcenter or on the web. Tips can be attached to cables with a crimping tool, which can be found in the Network Closet and should be guarded with your life!

When making a phone or ethernet cable, you must crimp the wires in the opposite order on the two ends.
