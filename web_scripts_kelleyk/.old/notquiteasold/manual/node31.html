Troubleshooting

Here is the list of questions you should go through when someone says ``Hey Netgeek, my internet's not working!'':
  • Is the ``link'' light on their ethernet card on?
  • If so, can you ping their computer?
  • If you can ping their computer from within the house, can you ping the gateway (18.237.0.1)? If the gateway is down, you will not be able to connect to computers outside of the house. The gateway is located in the student center and we have no control over it. Sometimes it just gets rebooted, and is online again within 15 minutes. If it is down for much longer than that, a call to Information Systems is in order.
  • If the gateway is up, perhaps the DNS server is down, or their DNS server is entered incorrectly in their networking settings.

Good troubleshooting will come with practice.
