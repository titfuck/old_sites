Straight from the Bible Belt, it’s Gus Blomquist ‘99! Having grown up the backwater town of Greensborough, NC, “FunGus” is well-versed in the sacred motto of southern humour:”Be wacky, Be unintelligable, Be loud.” Yet, like the track star he is, he knows how to party. Unaware of the dangers of McNuggets, Swedish girls, and water beds, our new Steward has had a few mishaps on his journey towards a degree in Biology. However, he still plans to continue on to Medical school and become the screwiest, shakiest, funkiest brain surgeon ever! “Look! I’m a bowl of lingenberry pudding!” “Shut up, Gus!”
