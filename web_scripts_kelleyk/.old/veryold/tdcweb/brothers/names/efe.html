From: Izmir, Turkey
Class:  '98
Major: Computer Science
Room:: Just Graduated

When he got off the plane, this crazy Turk couldn’t tell a Farfenuggen from an apple fritter, but after three years at MIT, Efe Cakarel ‘98 has evolved into one “damn smooth” dude. If everyone from Susuzde Turkey shared even half of Efe’s enthusiasm for life, eternal happiness and late seventies dress, one would definitely mark it on one’s list of places to visit. As a Computer Science major, a member of the crew and sailing teams, the president of the class of ‘98 and a spinner of disks, Efe is constantly in action. Yet between his slick moves and problem sets, Efe makes time for long distance phone calls to his home in Turkey conducted at ear splitting decibel levels at odd hours in the morning.
