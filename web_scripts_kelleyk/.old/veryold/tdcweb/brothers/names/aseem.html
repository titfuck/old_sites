Room:: The Gig

Bastion of teenage angst, Aseem Agarwala ‘98 (“Aga who!”) of Sanford Florida struggles to reconcile his Beat cogitations with the impending onset of the information infrastructure. When he’s not howling in his sleep to Ginsberg or Kerouac, this debative young Computer Science major can be found organising his color coded laundry baskets. With interests in the philosophy of the Liberalism and the anatomy of women (“you can look but you can’t touch”) he highlighted this year by getting an elbow and some knee while dancing. A newcomer to the party scene, Aseem made a lasting impression on us all with his unfettered enthusiasm at the annual Bondage party. Hopefully he’ll be able to sit down again by that time next year.
