What can we say about T.D.Cody? Well, he's a big dumb mutt hailing from the back alleys of Roxbury. Early in his TDC life, he enjoyed chewing on radioactive lead bricks, CD's, socks, homework, and pretty much anything he could get a hold of. Now after years of maturing, he only mauls anything plastic. As brother Geiger, and brother Hahn have found out, a dog is a chick magnet, even if it's as dumb as Cody. At parties Cody, majoring in Chasing Lemons, can be found keeping the peace, and livening up the place. He also takes care of any little spills, but don't let him clean up too many. Down Cody!!
