TDC has sports.  Directly across the street from our house is the MIT boathouse, and on the other side are the tennis courts and brand new lighted turf -- perfect for midnight pickup soccer.  Some of our brothers choose to play varsity sports, and most play a mix of intramurals.

We have varsity athletes in Cross Country, Indoor Track, and Outdoor Track.  We also have several brothers on the MIT Men's Soccer team.  A couple of guys have participated in crew and sailing at one time or another. To see more about varsity sports at MIT you can go to the MIT Athletics Home Page.

Our Athletic Chair, Phil Marfuta, puts together our intramural teams.  IM's are great for competing without the serious time commitment, and to get out on the field, play on a team with your brothers, and feel house pride.  Last winter Phil saw our B-league basketball team all the way to the finals.  Other sports we played last year were soccer, tennis, ice hockey, table tennis, pool, ultimate frisbee, and volleyball. To get a complete list of intramural sports you can go to the MIT Intramural's Homepage.
