From I-93 South

Go through Boston (on overpass). Take Cambridge/Storrow Drive exit near the FleetCenter. Take right lane to Cambridge. Take left at light, then bear right past Museum of Science. Take left onto Memorial Dr. Continue through underpasses until you see MIT Killian Court on right. Go under one more underpass. TDC is on 3rd corner. (See picture) We'll have the flag out.

Take left onto Memorial Dr. Continue through underpasses until you see MIT Killian Court on right. Go under one more underpass. TDC is on 3rd corner. (See picture) We'll have the flag out.

Continue straight on Memorial Dr. (go over hump after Microcenter). Once road splits, get in left lane and take first left (Careful!). We are right there (you'll see the flag) Check our pic.
