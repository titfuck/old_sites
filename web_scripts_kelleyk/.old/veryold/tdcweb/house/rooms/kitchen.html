The Kitchen

A truly state-of-the-art kitchen. Industrial design makes those slick appliances in yuppie kitchenware catalogs look downright flimsy. Renovated one year ago, the kitchen is in perfect condition. Our full-time cook raves on and on about the bright shiny surfaces. If you ever get those late-night muchies, you'll soon dicover the joy of having refridgrators stocked with a variety of meats, cheeses, vegatables and breads, easily sliced and assembled with the commerical meat slicer and topped with a little Grey Poupon. If your looking for something a little more formal, sample the delicious creations leftover from lunch or dinner stored in the People's Fridge.
