The Pipe

Da' dark den of devious deviants, the Pipe has traditionally been a smoky realm. Last term was no exception, with Patty's inhuman appetite for Marlboro Reds and Mike trying hard. The blue and yellow pipes, purple celing and questionable cartoons contribute to the thick atmosphere. However, Patty will tell you that you can't disscoitate from the house by merely hiding out it the haze. Squint your watering eyes and see if you can see the Chesire cat, fading away...
