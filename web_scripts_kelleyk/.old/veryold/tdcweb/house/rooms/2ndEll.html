The 2nd Ell

Currently the biggest single in the house, though you wouldn't know it from the drawing, the 2nd Ell is a safe haven for very large Canadian's. Well, just one really, Ollie. This room traditionally holds 2-3 people in its large area (including a huge loft). It's windows overlook the courtyard and the 2nd floor deck, allowing easy access to deck parties all year 'round.
