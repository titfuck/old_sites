The Urinal

Although its called the Urinal, this room doesn't smell, unless you count the vapors wafting in from the Breadbox. But boy does it ROCK! If you like homebrewed college wuss-rock that is. But seriously folks, Eric really likes it here, and has managed to turn it into a resonably professionally recording studio. After an intensive week-long recording session early in the year, Eric is putting the finishing touches on his first CD release. It would have been done sooner, but a surge of mental ecstasy fried his circuit boards and scrambled his drum tracks.
